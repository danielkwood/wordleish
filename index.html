Wordle [clone]

Guess the word in six attempts. Each attempt must be a valid five-letter word. Click button to submit.
After each attempt, the colour of the letter tiles will change to show how close your guess was to the word.

A green tile means the letter is in the correct spot.
An orange tile means the letter is in the word but not in the correct spot.
A white tile means the letter is not in the word in any spot.
